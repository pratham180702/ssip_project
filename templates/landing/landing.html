The Complaint and Decision Making System by AMC

This system is designed for the people of Ahmedabad, to solve their complaints in a very efficient manner with the help of technology. The process of resolving complaints is very slow due to many reasons one of them being the complaint not reaching the right authority in time, this system solves that problem by directly posting people's complaints to the right authority in time.

Get Started
Proccess Of filing complaint

Registration

Register yourself on the AMC complaint management system, fill in the required details

File your Complaint

After registering yourself on the portal, make a new complaint. select the sector, and the category of your complaint, and description or image about your complaint

Get updates

You will get updates about the status of your complaint with description of what is happening with your complaint

Complaint Resolved

Your complaint will be reviwed and resolved by the AMC officals in given timelimit
